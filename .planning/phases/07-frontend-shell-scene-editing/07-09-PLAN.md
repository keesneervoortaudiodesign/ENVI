<objective>
Guarantee scene integrity and persistence (SC2 + SC4 persistence): draw-time ground-zone topology validation with hard reject (D-07), the persistent click-to-select validation panel for non-geometric issues (WEB-04), debounced committed-edit autosave with flush-on-unload (D-04), the dirty/saved project-bar indicator, and the typed-name Delete-project confirmation. This closes the "always-valid ground-zone topology" invariant and makes edits durable.

Purpose: a partially crossing ground zone corrupts the acoustic ground model; hard-rejecting at draw time keeps topology always valid, while non-geometric issues on real objects surface in a separate persistent panel (D-07 resolves the SC2 ambiguity). Autosave must never put the network in the drag loop (D-04).
Output: `validate/groundZone.ts`, `panels/ValidationPanel.tsx`, `panels/RejectBanner.tsx`, `store/autosave.ts`, `panels/ProjectBar.tsx` (dirty indicator), `panels/DeleteProjectDialog.tsx`, a validation E2E.
