<objective>
Expose the two new backend seams as thin `envi-service` endpoints (D-05, D-08): `POST /api/v1/meta/interpolate-spectrum` (delegates to the `envi-store` interpolation core from 07-01) and `POST /api/v1/dgm/triangulate` (delegates to the `envi-dgm` TIN from 07-02), each mapping typed store/dgm errors to structured `ApiError` 4xx bodies. Acoustics and geometry stay OUT of the service layer (SVC-07).

Purpose: the editor's live preview and `PUT /scene` validation must share ONE Rust interpolation impl (D-05); the DGM must be reachable server-side without panicking on hostile input (D-08 / Pitfall 3). This plan wires the handlers, routes, and error mapping and proves them with oneshot contract tests.
Output: `interpolate_spectrum` handler + `InterpolateReq/Resp`, `dgm.rs` handler + `DgmReq/Resp`, `From<DgmError> for ApiError`, two route registrations, two contract tests.
</objective>
