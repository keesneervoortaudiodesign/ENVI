<objective>
Create the **`envi-geo`** crate — the milestone's ONE reprojection boundary (GEOX-04, D-03): pure-Rust
WGS84 lon/lat <-> project-local UTM meters on **proj4rs 0.1.10** (D-01 — zero C toolchain, no proj.db,
no GDAL_DATA; the C `gdal`/`proj` stack is deferred to Phase 8 per D-02). The crate owns the `LonLat`/
`SceneXY` newtypes, `to_utm`/`to_wgs84`, auto-UTM-zone selection, and the SC3 loud rejection of
degree-magnitude scene coordinates. `envi-store` (plan 06-02), `envi-service` (plans 06-03/04), and
later `envi-gis` (Phase 8) all import this crate — no other crate may call proj4rs.

proj4rs's one sharp edge — longlat coordinates are **radians** (06-RESEARCH Pitfall 1) — is quarantined
inside `transform.rs`: conversion happens there and ONLY there; the public API speaks degrees and meters.

Purpose: every scene coordinate in the entire milestone flows through this seam; the D-08 startup
self-check (plan 06-03) and the SC3 landmark accuracy criterion ride directly on it.
Output: the `envi-geo` crate (newtypes, zone selection, transforms, typed errors), the pyproj oracle
fixture + generator (house oracle pattern), and the full unit-test ladder.
</objective>
