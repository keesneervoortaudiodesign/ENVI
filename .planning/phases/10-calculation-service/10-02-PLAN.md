<objective>
Make the served application **cross-origin isolated** so the browser exposes `SharedArrayBuffer` (the prerequisite for the wasm-bindgen-rayon thread pool the rest of Phase 10 needs). This is the only server-side surface the client-side solve requires: two response headers on the delivery server plus the matching dev-server headers (D-04). Real authentication is explicitly out of scope (D-12).

Purpose: Without `COOP: same-origin` + `COEP: credentialless` on the top-level document, `self.crossOriginIsolated` is false and `initThreadPool` cannot spawn workers — the entire threaded solve is dead on arrival. `credentialless` (not `require-corp`) is chosen so the Phase-8 direct GIS/basemap fetches keep working.

Output: COOP/COEP layers on the `envi-service` bundle response and the equivalent `server.headers` in `web/vite.config.ts`.
</objective>
