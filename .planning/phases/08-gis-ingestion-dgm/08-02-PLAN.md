<objective>
Create the new pure-Rust, sans-I/O `envi-gis` crate and its COG decode core: parse TIFF/BigTIFF IFDs,
derive the geotransform from geo-tags, and decode a windowed region of a cached whole tile into an
`f32` raster — all synchronous, over byte slices, natively `cargo test`-able against committed
GDAL-generated fixtures.

Purpose: this is the load-bearing, security-critical foundation of the import pipeline. Whole-tile
ingest (08-07) hands cached bytes here; feature construction (08-04) windows and decimates through it.
Sans-I/O keeps the whole core testable and WASM-safe.
Output: `envi-gis` crate scaffold + `GisError` + `cog/{header,window,geo_tags}` + committed COG
fixtures + fixture tests.
</objective>
