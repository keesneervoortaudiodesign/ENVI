<objective>
Prove the simplest full-stack path end-to-end and complete the Walking Skeleton: geometrical divergence (AV 1106/07 Eq. 330, ENG-01) and ISO 9613-1 air absorption with the Nord2000 Eq. 287 band correction (ENG-04), evaluated as complex values at all 105 1/12-octave points, driven by a point sub-source with a per-1/12-octave source spectrum (SRC-01) — validated through the harness against analytic references, turning the free-field cases green.

Purpose: This is the seed of the project's load-bearing output contract. The complex convention defined here (e^{+jωt}, phase −ωτ with τ = R/c as the carried primitive, |H| normalized so L_p = L_W + 20·log10|H|) is what Phase 2 multiplies ground/diffraction ratios into, Phase 3 replaces τ-differences in, and Phase 4 stacks into H[s,r,f]. Retrofitting phase later is the RESEARCH-flagged anti-pattern; it goes in now, tested by cancellation.

Output: `envi-engine::propagation` (divergence, air_absorption, direct_path), `envi-engine::transfer` (TransferSpectrum + TransferTensor alias + the convention contract in doc comments), free-field capability live in the harness, two green free-field cases.
</objective>
