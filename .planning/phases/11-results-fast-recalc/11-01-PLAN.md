<objective>
Build the pure-Rust/WASM readout foundation every Phase-11 surface reads from: (1) net-new A/C weighting tables at the 105 exact 1/12-octave centres (D-09), (2) the OPFS tensor reader that decodes the Phase-10 write-only chunk store back into arrays, and (3) the readout orchestration that drives the FORCE-validated engine laws (`readout_coherent`/`readout_incoherent`) over the decoded tensor to produce per-receiver band levels, dB(A)/dB(C) totals, and the coherent/incoherent split.
