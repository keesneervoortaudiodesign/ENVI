<objective>
Wire the plan-05-02 min-phase kernel into the one solve path (ENG-10, SC2/SC3/SC4): the
transmitted straight-through term joins the diffracted/reflected field as complex pressure inside
propagation/, native-side, pre-conj (D-05) — realized at `screen_channel`, the single assembly
point covering SM4/5/6 — with D-10 structural gating (`None` never constructs the term) pinned by
a permanent bit-exact opaque regression. Then close the phase's documentation contract (D-07):
REQUIREMENTS/README/module headers updated for both extensions, and the deferred Fs seam recorded.

**Composition-point resolution (per 05-RESEARCH Finding 4, planner-confirmed):** every screen
sub-model's h_coh_factor is a ratio relative to the free-field direct p̂₀, and the transmitted
path travels the same S→R line (ray direction preserved) — so the transmitted term relative to p̂₀
is exactly T(f): `h_semi = h_opaque + T_native[band]`, one complex addition at `screen_channel`
AFTER the sub-model call. This point (rather than inside run_four_path/run_eight_path) is
load-bearing twice over: it covers all three screen classes at one code point, and it keeps T
OUTSIDE the F-coherence weighting — the min-phase filter is deterministic and fully coherent, so
it must not be decorrelated by F or leak into p_incoh. No extra ground-weighting factor applies to
the straight-through term (the Eq. 332 ground interaction lives in the sub-model results T adds
to); in the marginal transition zone the whole screen branch — including T — is weighted by
r_scr1, which is correct (leakage only exists where the screen does) and documented, not "fixed".

SC4 (per-façade building transmission) collapses into this same mechanism (D-11): the engine
applies whichever crossed partition's R(f) the job carries; façade selection is Phase-7/9 logic.

Purpose: semi-transparent screens/façades become computable while the validated opaque engine is
provably untouched.
Output: threaded seam (SolveJob.isolation → terrain_effect → screen_channel), opaque bit-exact
regression (T6) + T7/T8/T9, and the phase close-out docs.
</objective>
