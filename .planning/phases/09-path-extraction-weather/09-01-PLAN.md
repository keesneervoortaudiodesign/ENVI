<objective>
Deliver the first two links of the GIS→engine geometry pipeline as pure-Rust, WASM-safe `envi-gis` modules: the DEM cut-profile extractor (GEOX-01) that turns a source→receiver line over the imported DGM TIN into the `(x,z)` ground points of an `envi_engine::scene::TerrainProfile`, and the impedance segmentation (GEOX-02) that assigns each profile interval a `GroundSegment` by resolving drawn > imported > default.

Purpose: These are the load-bearing inputs every real `SolveJob` consumes. GEOX-01 is the single self-build the phase pins with an offline oracle; GEOX-02 fixes the ground-reflection segmentation that silently shifts every reflection if it is off-by-one.
Output: `crates/envi-gis/src/{profile,impedance}.rs`, a committed r.profile-faithful oracle fixture + test, `mod` declarations and new `GisError` variants in `lib.rs`.
</objective>
